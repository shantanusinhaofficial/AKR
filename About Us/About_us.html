ABOUT US

Welcome to The AKR Company, a Patna-based start-up that is audaciously revolutionizing the unorganized retail industries in India's FMCG, Clothing, and Footwear sectors.

Established as Audacious Kingship Raiders Private Limited, we were selected by the Bihar government under the start-up policy scheme and nurtured by the Bihar Industries Association's Venture Park Incubator.

What began as a vision conceived by two undergraduate students has now blossomed into a dynamic venture led by a team of six dedicated co-founders.

At The AKR Company, we are thrilled to announce the launch of our innovative B2B solutions, tailored to empower small businesses in the unorganized retail sector across India. Our cutting-edge solutions are strategically crafted to equip these businesses with the necessary resources to thrive in today's fiercely competitive market.

We aim to enhance their supply chain management, boost operational efficiency, minimize costs, and deliver products of exceptional quality to their valued customers.

The AKR Company's B2B solutions have been meticulously developed to empower small businesses operating in unorganized retail industries across India. We prioritize excellence, efficiency, and innovation, ensuring that our clients have the tools they need to thrive.
